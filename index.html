The Location of Canada

Canada is a country located in North America, stretching from the Atlantic Ocean in the east to the Pacific Ocean in the west. It is the second largest country in the world by land area, after Russia, and shares land borders with the United States to the south and northwest (Alaska). Canada is known for its diverse geography, which includes vast forests, prairies, mountains, and Arctic tundra. The country is divided into ten provinces and three territories, each with their own distinct landscapes and cultures. The capital city of Canada is Ottawa, located in the southeastern province of Ontario.
